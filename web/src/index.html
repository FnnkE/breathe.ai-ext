What is breathe.ai?

breathe.ai is a web platform dedicated to making health care services more efficient. The platform offers a variety of tools including electronic medical record storage and AI assistance. When feeling under the weather, the AI (powered by Claude) can help you schedule an appointment while collecting basic information to pass onto the doctor. When at the appointment, the health professionals can access your information as well as see what the AI suggests looking out for. As either a patient or worker, you should not use the advice from the AI to make important decisions.
